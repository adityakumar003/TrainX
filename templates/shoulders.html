Shoulder Training

BUILD STRONG, ROUNDED, AND STABLE SHOULDERS

Strong, well-developed shoulders create the foundation of a powerful and aesthetic upper body. Shoulder workouts primarily target the three deltoid heads — anterior (front), lateral (side), and posterior (rear) — along with the trapezius and rotator cuff muscles for stability. Compound lifts like the overhead press and push press build mass and strength, while isolation moves such as lateral raises, front raises, and reverse flyes enhance definition and symmetry. Balanced shoulder training improves posture, enhances performance in other lifts, and reduces the risk of injuries by supporting joint stability and mobility.
