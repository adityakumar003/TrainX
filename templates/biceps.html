Biceps Training

BUILD A STRONG AND POWERFUL BICEPS

Biceps exercises primarily focus on the biceps brachii, brachialis, and brachioradialis muscles. Incorporating a variety of barbell, dumbbell, cable, and bodyweight exercises ensures maximum growth, peak definition, and arm symmetry. Movements like barbell curls, hammer curls, and incline dumbbell curls emphasize both the long and short heads of the biceps, while isolation exercises like concentration curls and preacher curls allow for full contraction and focus. Proper form, progressive overload, and controlled motion are key to achieving strong, well-defined arms while preventing injury.
